Our Programs

PILLARS TUTORIALS offers a wide range of academic programs that give the students the support that they need to achieve.
The basic programs are the Daily Pillars, Strengthening Pillars, and Heightening Pillars. These programs are offered to students from Preschool to Senior High School.
DAILY PILARS

This program is designed for students who need guidance with their daily homework. It is ideal for students who are still in the process of building their study habits, or who need help in preparing for daily tests in school. Students may choose to have their sessions one-on-one, or in groups of 2-4. We offer Daily Pillars for all school subjects, or only for specific subjects. But don’t expect your tutors to do the work for you! Daily Pillars is a healthy mix of guided and independent work.

STRENGTHENING PILLARS

Having trouble in Math or English? Then Strengthening Pillars is for you. This program is designed to help students in subject areas where remediation is needed. It aims to help students recover prerequisite skills, and to bring them to the level of skill at par with his current grade level. We offer Strengthening Pillars in the following skills/subject areas:

  • Math (All subjects)
  • Science (General Science, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics)
  • English (Reading, Writing, Speaking, and Listening)

An initial assessment is administered to determine the student’s current skill level. The results will then be used in designing the best-fit program for the student. All Strengthening Pillars sessions are one-on-one.

ENRICHING PILLARS

In Pillars, we know that even students who are doing well in school need support, too. The Enriching Pillars Program is designed for students who would like to be a step ahead in the lessons, or who would like to have the chance to participate in academic competitions. With programs designed by seasoned teachers and a trainer of former International Math Olympiads, allow us to guide you to soar to greater academic heights!

  • Math: Advanced Math & Math Contest Training
  • English: Speed Reading, Preparation for Speaking and Writing Competitions
TERTIARY PILLARS

This program is designed for College students who would like to maintain a high average, or who need help with certain subjects. We offer collge tutorials in Math, Science, and Accounting. We know that college students lead a busy and sometimes stressful lifestyle, and our goal is to find the program and schedule that works best for you. We offer Reiki healing to help you deal with stress too!

BRIDGING PILLARS

Senior High School subjects are new additions to the High School curriculum. We understand that students who come from different Junior High School programs may have difficulty adjusting to a new environment and new content. This program offers a way to bridge the gap between Junior High and Senior High. Recover needed prerequisite skills, and get a refresher course on some crucial Senior High School subjects. Bridging Pillars are conducted as group or lecture sessions on the following subjects:

  • General Mathematics
  • Precalculus
  • Basic Calculus
  • Statistics and Probability
  • Business Math
  • Entrepreneurship

Other Program Offerings

Pillars offers a variety of other programs for the holistic development of the students.
ENTRANCE & PLACEMENT PILLARS

Benjamin Franklin hit the nail on the head when he said, “By failing to prepare, you are preparing to fail.” When it comes to something as life-changing as getting into a good university, college, or senior high school, nothing beats good preparation. In Pillars, we offer college review programs aligned with the Ateneo College Entrance Test (ACET) and UP College Entrance Test (UPCAT), as well as the Ateneo Senior High School Aptitude and Placement Exam (ASHAPE). Take practice tests and review high school lessons, and face those entrance tests with greater confidence!

WORD PILLARS

  • Chinese
  • Korean
